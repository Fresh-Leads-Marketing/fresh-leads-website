## SECTION 5: EMAIL & SMS MARKETING

**Q: How does email and SMS marketing work for laundromats?**
A: We use your customer data to send targeted email and SMS campaigns that bring people back through your doors. This includes welcome sequences for new customers, win-back campaigns for people who haven't visited in a while, promotional offers, and automated review requests.

**Q: Where do you get the customer data for email and SMS?**
A: We pull customer data directly from your laundry POS system via API integration. Systems like Cents, LaundroWorks, CCI, and CleanCloud allow us to sync customer contact information, visit history, and transaction data automatically. This API connection is required for our email and SMS services to work.

**Q: How do re-engagement campaigns work?**
A: Our system automatically identifies customers the moment they fall outside certain conditions, such as not visiting in over 60 days. Once flagged, those customers are automatically targeted with personalized email and SMS messaging designed to bring them back in. It runs continuously across your entire customer base, so no lapsed customer slips through the cracks.

**Q: Will customers get annoyed by too many messages?**
A: No. We're strategic about frequency and targeting. We don't blast your entire list with the same message. Instead, we segment customers based on behavior (new customers, regulars, lapsed visitors) and send relevant messages at the right time. Our unsubscribe rates are consistently well below industry averages because the messages people receive are actually useful to them.

**Q: Do I need to write the emails or texts?**
A: No. We write all the copy, design the emails, and schedule everything. You don't have to do anything. We'll share the campaigns with you before they go out if you'd like to review them, but most clients trust us to handle it.

## SECTION 6: CRM & POS INTEGRATION

**Q: What is a CRM and why do I need one?**
A: A CRM (Customer Relationship Management) system is a central dashboard where all your customer data lives. It shows you who's visiting, who's lapsed, who's a VIP, and what marketing they've responded to. Without a CRM, your customer data is scattered across your POS, your email, your phone, and maybe a spreadsheet. A CRM brings it all together so you can make smarter marketing decisions and automate follow-ups.

**Q: Does your CRM connect to my POS system?**
A: Yes. We connect directly to your laundry POS system via API so that customer data syncs automatically. New customer sign-ups, visit history, and transaction data flow into your CRM in real time. No manual imports, no spreadsheets. Supported systems include Cents, LaundroWorks, CCI, CleanCloud, and others.

**Q: Do I need to manage the CRM myself?**
A: No. We handle everything. Setup, configuration, customer segmentation, automated campaign triggers, and ongoing maintenance are all managed by our team. You can log in and view your dashboard anytime, but you never need to touch it for it to work.

**Q: What POS systems do you integrate with?**
A: We work with most major laundry POS systems including Cents, LaundroWorks, CCI, and CleanCloud. If your system offers API access, we can likely connect to it. During onboarding, we'll confirm compatibility and get the integration set up.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**
